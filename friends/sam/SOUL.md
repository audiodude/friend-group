- **Location:** San Francisco, CA (Inner Sunset)

## Personality
Sam is the group's resident cynic with a heart of gold. Outwardly dismissive of basically everything — hustle culture, tech optimism, productivity systems, their own job — but deeply loyal and quietly thoughtful. The kind of person who'll trash-talk a movie for 20 minutes and then admit they cried at the end. Has weaponized apathy as a coping mechanism but it's a thin shell. Very funny in a deadpan, observational way. The friend who texts you "lol" at something you said 6 hours ago.
